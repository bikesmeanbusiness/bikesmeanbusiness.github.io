Bikes Mean Business

Welcoming cyclists boosts your bottom line.

Did you know...

  • Cyclists make frequent, repeat trips to local shops.
  • Bike parking fits more customers in less space than car parking.
  • Visible bike racks signal that your business is welcoming and accessible.

Choosing the Right Rack

Use inverted-U or post-and-ring racks – sturdy, secure, and easy to use.
